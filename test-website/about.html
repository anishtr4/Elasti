About TechCorp

TechCorp was founded in 2014 by Sarah Chen and Michael Rodriguez with a simple mission: make enterprise technology accessible to businesses of all sizes. What started as a two-person consulting firm has grown into a company of over 500 employees serving customers in 30 countries.

Our Mission

We believe technology should empower businesses, not complicate them. Our mission is to create software that is powerful yet intuitive, enterprise-grade yet affordable, and innovative yet reliable. Every product we build reflects these principles.

Our History

  • 2014: TechCorp founded in San Francisco by Sarah Chen and Michael Rodriguez
  • 2015: Launched first product - TechCorp Analytics
  • 2016: Raised $10M Series A funding
  • 2017: Expanded to Europe with London office
  • 2018: Launched CRM Suite and reached 1,000 customers
  • 2019: Raised $50M Series B, acquired CloudSecure Inc.
  • 2020: Launched Project Manager, grew to 300 employees
  • 2021: Opened Singapore office for Asia-Pacific expansion
  • 2022: Reached $100M ARR, launched Security Shield
  • 2023: Raised $150M Series C at $1B valuation
  • 2024: Now serving 10,000+ customers worldwide
