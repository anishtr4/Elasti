Frequently Asked Questions

General Questions

What is TechCorp?

TechCorp is a software company that provides enterprise solutions including analytics, CRM, project management, and security tools. We serve over 10,000 customers in 30 countries.

Where is TechCorp located?

Our headquarters is in San Francisco, California. We also have offices in London, Singapore, Austin, and New York City.

How long has TechCorp been in business?

TechCorp was founded in 2014, so we've been serving customers for over 10 years.

Products & Pricing

How much does TechCorp Analytics Pro cost?

TechCorp Analytics Pro costs $99 per month per user. We offer discounts for annual billing (2 months free) and volume licensing for teams over 50 users.

What is the cheapest TechCorp product?

TechCorp Project Manager is our most affordable product at $29 per month per user. It includes all core project management features including Kanban boards, time tracking, and team collaboration.

Do you offer a free trial?

Yes! All our products come with a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your monthly subscription at any time. Your access will continue until the end of your current billing period. Annual subscriptions can be canceled with a pro-rated refund within the first 30 days.

Yes, we offer comprehensive training programs including on-demand video tutorials, live webinars, and in-person workshops. Training sessions start at $5,000 per day for on-site training.

Security & Compliance

Is my data secure with TechCorp?

Absolutely. We use industry-leading security measures including AES-256 encryption at rest and in transit, regular penetration testing, and 24/7 security monitoring. We're SOC 2 Type II certified.

Are you GDPR compliant?

Yes, TechCorp is fully GDPR compliant. We have a Data Protection Officer, provide data processing agreements, and give you tools to handle data subject requests.

Where is my data stored?

By default, data is stored in our US data centers. EU customers can choose to have their data stored in our EU data centers (located in Ireland and Germany). We also support data residency requirements for other regions.

Integration & API

What integrations do you support?

We integrate with over 100 popular business tools including Salesforce, HubSpot, Slack, Microsoft 365, Google Workspace, Jira, GitHub, and many more. See our integrations page for the full list.

Do you have an API?

Yes, all our products have REST APIs with comprehensive documentation. API access is included in all paid plans. We also offer webhook support for real-time event notifications.
