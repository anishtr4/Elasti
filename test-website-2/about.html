Our Story

Founded in 2010, GreenGarden Landscaping started with a single truck and a passion for sustainable horticulture. Today, we are the leading provider of eco-friendly landscaping services in the Springfield metro area.

Our mission is simple: To create beautiful outdoor spaces that are in harmony with nature. We verify that every product we use is safe for the environment, pets, and your family.

Our Certifications

We are proud members of the National Association of Landscape Professionals (NALP) and are Certified Organic Land Care Professionals.
