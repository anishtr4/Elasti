Do you offer free estimates?
Yes! We offer free on-site estimates for all our services within a 20-mile radius of Springfield. Contact us to schedule an appointment.
What areas do you serve?
We currently serve Springfield, Shelbyville, and Capital City. For large commercial projects, we travel statewide.
Are your fertilizers safe for pets?
Absolutely. We use 100% organic, OMRI-listed fertilizers that are safe for pets, children, and local waterways immediately after application.
How often should I water my new plants?
New plantings typically require deep watering 2-3 times per week for the first season. We provide detailed care instructions with every installation.
Do you do snow removal in the winter?
Yes, we offer commercial snow plowing and salting services from November through March.
